Bottom line: a candidate for consolidating tasks, documents, time and dashboards in one workspace

Decision summary

ClickUp

ClickUp combines tasks, Docs, Kanban, calendars, forms, time tracking, dashboards and chat in one workspace. Free Forever lists unlimited tasks and free-plan members, but includes 60 MB of storage and one form. Unlimited expands storage, forms, Gantt, integrations, time tracking and permission-controlled guests.

Advantages confirmed from official sources

  • Free lists unlimited tasks and free-plan members
  • Tasks, Docs, Kanban, calendars and a form can be tested in one workspace
  • Unlimited expands storage, forms, Gantt, integrations, time tracking and permission-controlled guests
  • Enterprise adds custom roles, SAML, SCIM, audit logs and data residency

Constraints to confirm before purchase

  • Free is limited to 60 MB of storage and one form
  • Upgrading requires the entire Workspace rather than a single member
  • The large feature set requires a deliberately limited operating model
  • AI uses separate plan and credit structures that should be priced independently

When it may fit

Suitable when

You want to consolidate several work functions in one workspace and gradually add views, forms, time tracking and automation.

Consider alternatives when

Consider a simpler Kanban tool when basic task sharing is sufficient or administration must remain minimal.

Frequently asked questions

How many people can use ClickUp Free?

Free Forever lists unlimited free-plan members and unlimited tasks, with limits such as 60 MB of storage and one form.

Can one person upgrade separately?

ClickUp’s pricing FAQ states that upgrading requires the entire Workspace, meaning all members in that Workspace.

What security certifications does ClickUp list?

The official security page lists SOC 1 Type 2, SOC 2 Type 2, SOC 3 and ISO 27001-related certifications and states that customer data is hosted on AWS.
