Bottom line: a candidate for managing documents, databases and tasks in one workspace

Decision summary

Notion

Notion combines pages, databases, documents, forms, sites and projects in one workspace. The Free plan provides unlimited pages and blocks for individuals, while multi-member free workspaces have a block limit. Team buyers should separately evaluate paid-member seats, guests, page permissions, teamspaces and AI data-retention conditions.

Advantages confirmed from official sources

  • Documents, databases, tasks and forms can share one information model
  • Individuals can start on Free, which lists up to 10 external guests
  • Plus expands collaborative blocks, uploads and guests
  • Business adds SSO and granular database permissions; Enterprise adds SCIM and audit logs

Constraints to confirm before purchase

  • Free multi-member workspaces have a block limit
  • Members are paid seats, while guests are a separate page-level access role
  • Broader access granted elsewhere can override an intended restriction
  • AI, custom domains and advanced administration may require paid plans or add-on charges

When it may fit

Suitable when

You want company knowledge, meeting notes, manuals, project databases and tasks in one adaptable workspace.

Consider alternatives when

Consider a specialist project-management tool when Gantt, resource planning or simpler strict permissions are the primary requirement.

Frequently asked questions

Can a team use Notion for free?

Yes, but free workspaces with two or more members have a block limit. Free also lists 5 MB per file and 10 external guests.

Are guests paid seats?

The pricing page states that guests are free, but they only access individual pages to which they are invited and are distinct from workspace members.

Can data be exported?

Notion documents workspace exports to HTML, Markdown and CSV. Confirm plan conditions for bulk PDF export and advanced retention controls.
