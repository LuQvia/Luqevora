Decision summary

Trello

Free supports up to 10 collaborators and 10 boards per Workspace. Standard adds unlimited boards, custom fields, advanced checklists and card mirroring. Premium adds multiple views, unlimited automation, administrative and security features, observers and CSV export.

Confirmed advantages

  • Free includes unlimited cards and Power-Ups
  • Standard adds unlimited boards, 250MB attachments, custom fields and 1,000 monthly automation runs
  • Premium provides calendar, timeline, table, dashboard and map views
  • Enterprise adds organization-wide permissions, multiple Workspaces, SSO and user provisioning

Constraints to verify

  • Every member of a Free Workspace is an administrator
  • Free is limited to 10 collaborators, 10 boards and 10MB per attachment
  • CSV board export is documented for Premium
  • Multi-board guests and organization-wide controls require Enterprise review

Administrator and guest considerations

All members of a Free Workspace are administrators and can invite people and change some Workspace settings. Standard and Premium separate administrator and normal-member roles, while Premium adds further controls over board creation, deletion and guest invitations.

Standard includes single-board guests. The billing treatment of external users on multiple boards should be checked before rollout. Enterprise documents multi-board guests.

Exports and portability

Atlassian support documents CSV board export for Trello Premium. For migration or backup, test whether attachments, comments and Power-Up data required by your process are included in the available export.

When it may fit

Consider Trello when

You want a straightforward card-moving workflow with limited initial configuration for marketing, production, hiring, personal work or simple project tracking.

Compare alternatives when

You need strict role separation on a free plan, complex dependencies and resource planning, detailed cross-project reporting, or a document/database platform as the primary system.

Pre-purchase checklist

  • Confirm collaborator and board counts
  • Decide whether all-admin Free workspaces are acceptable
  • Estimate automation command volume and attachment size
  • Review single-board versus multi-board guest treatment
  • Confirm whether Premium views are required
  • Check the plans for CSV export and SSO

Frequently asked questions

How many people can use Trello Free?

The Free plan supports up to 10 collaborators and 10 boards per Workspace.

Can Free separate administrators and normal members?

Official support says every member of a Free Workspace is an administrator. Role separation should be reviewed on paid plans.

Can Trello export CSV?

Trello Premium documents board information export in CSV format.
