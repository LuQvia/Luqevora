Pricing and plan limits

Cost varies by provider, server resources, bandwidth, storage, backups, CDN, support, and add-ons. Allocate server cost across every hosted site.

For Cloudways, identify the lowest plan containing every required feature and compare the total for the full billing period.

  • Record introductory and renewal pricing separately
  • Compare monthly, annual, and longer-term totals
  • Check limits for seats, storage, sites, sends, or usage
  • Review refund, cancellation, and data-retention terms

Strengths and trade-offs

The platform offers more control than shared hosting, but domains, email, and some services may remain separate and require technical ownership.

  • Strength: Flexible cloud configuration
  • Strength: Developer and multi-site workflows
  • Verify: More operational decisions than shared hosting
  • Verify: Add-on, backup, and bandwidth cost

Who it suits

It fits teams with a technical owner who can size resources and operate several applications.

  • WordPress agencies
  • Developers managing several sites
  • Teams moving from unmanaged cloud servers

Implementation checklist

Pilot Cloudways in a small scope and document cost, workflow, migration, and administration before rollout.

  • Define the objective and completion criteria in one sentence
  • Reproduce a representative task in a free plan or trial
  • Test administrator, contributor, and viewer permissions
  • Perform a real backup or export test
  • Record the renewal date and internal owner
