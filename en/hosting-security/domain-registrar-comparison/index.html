Pricing and plan limits

Build a multi-year total for each TLD including registration, renewal, transfer, privacy, premium-domain rules, tax, and expiration recovery.

For domain registrars, identify the lowest plan containing every required feature and compare the total for the full billing period.

  • Record introductory and renewal pricing separately
  • Compare monthly, annual, and longer-term totals
  • Check limits for seats, storage, sites, sends, or usage
  • Review refund, cancellation, and data-retention terms

Strengths and trade-offs

Combining registrar, DNS, email, and hosting reduces accounts but enlarges the impact of one compromise or migration.

  • Strength: A criteria-based selection reduces future transfer work
  • Strength: Separating domain ownership from web hosting can improve portability
  • Verify: Promotional registration pricing
  • Verify: Ownership records, recovery access, and automatic renewal payment

Who it suits

It applies to every organization that treats its domain as a critical business asset.

  • New domains
  • Consolidating domains from several registrars
  • Businesses formalizing domain security and ownership

Implementation checklist

Pilot domain registrars in a small scope and document cost, workflow, migration, and administration before rollout.

  • Define the objective and completion criteria in one sentence
  • Reproduce a representative task in a free plan or trial
  • Test administrator, contributor, and viewer permissions
  • Perform a real backup or export test
  • Record the renewal date and internal owner
