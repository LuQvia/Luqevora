Why unofficial coupon pages require caution

Third-party coupon pages can contain expired codes, restricted offers, or terms that are not visible until checkout. Compare the official monthly and annual totals and confirm the final charge before purchase.

Budget rule: include sites, visits or bandwidth, storage, CDN, backup retention, and overage charges—not only the headline discount.

How to compare monthly and annual cost

  1. 1.Estimate the number of WordPress installs.
  2. 2.Choose visits-based or bandwidth-based billing.
  3. 3.Compare twelve monthly payments with the annual invoice.
  4. 4.Add storage, CDN, and possible overages.
  5. 5.Include any backup or environment add-ons.

When a discount should not drive the decision

Kinsta may fit

  • You value managed WordPress operations and expert support.
  • You want migrations, backups, WAF, CDN, and APM in one service.
  • You can handle annual upfront billing.

Compare alternatives

  • You mainly need the lowest-cost shared hosting.
  • You want bundled email and domain registration.
  • You prefer a plan without usage-based overages.
