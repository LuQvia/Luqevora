Conclusion: specialist managed WordPress operations, requiring planned bandwidth, visits, overages and retention

Decision summary

Kinsta

Kinsta is a managed cloud platform dedicated to WordPress. At the time checked, Single 20GB is $35 monthly or $350 annually and lists one WordPress install, 20GB server bandwidth, 10GB storage, 125GB CDN and 14 days of backup retention. It costs more than shared hosting, so verify the value of the included operations and limits.

Separate daily, manual, downloadable, external and hourly backups

Standard backups

Daily backups are included, and up to five manual backups can be created. The checked Single 20GB class lists 14 days of retention.

Portability and higher frequency

A downloadable backup can be generated once per week and must be downloaded within 48 hours. External S3/Google Cloud and hourly/six-hour backups are paid add-ons.

Limit note: Kinsta states that sites remain online after exceeding bandwidth, visits, storage or CDN limits, but usage-based overage fees apply.

Frequently asked questions

Is Kinsta free to use?

There is no permanent free plan. At the time checked, eligible plans for new customers include a first month free, followed by paid billing; a 30-day money-back guarantee is also stated.

What is included in Single 20GB?

It lists one WordPress install, 20GB server bandwidth, 10GB storage, 125GB CDN bandwidth, unlimited free migrations and 14 days of backup retention.

Can I download a backup to my computer?

Kinsta documentation states that a downloadable backup can be generated once per week and must be downloaded within 48 hours after creation.
