RoboForm Pricing: Free vs Premium vs Family, Renewal Cost and Discounts

RoboForm’s monthly equivalents are easy to read, but the real decision depends on annual billing, renewal price, device access and whether one or five Premium accounts are needed.

Bottom line

For one person using several devices, Premium is the default paid choice. For up to five people who need separate accounts, Family can be cheaper than buying multiple Premium subscriptions. Free is useful for testing or one-device use.

Budget from the renewal price, not only the first-year promotion. The official page displayed $29.88 per year for Premium renewal and $47.75 per year for Family renewal on July 24, 2026.

What the paid plans add

Premium includes the free features plus access on all devices, web access, secure shared folders, Emergency Access, breach monitoring, local-only mode and priority support. Family packages five separate Premium accounts under unified billing.

Do not pay for Family merely to share one login. Separate accounts preserve each person’s private vault while allowing selected items or folders to be shared.

How to calculate the real annual cost

  1. Record the first payment shown at checkout.
  2. Record the stated annual renewal price.
  3. Add tax and currency conversion costs.
  4. Decide how many separate accounts are required.
  5. Compare at least two years, because a first-year discount can distort the result.
A monthly equivalent is not a monthly cancellation promise. RoboForm displays these personal paid plans as annually billed.

Discounts and promotional pages

RoboForm maintains an official coupon page that has listed new-subscription, multi-year, student and military offers. Eligibility and discount size can change, and some offers may use separate landing pages.

Use only the provider’s current promotion and checkout terms. Do not rely on expired coupon codes or third-party claims.

Refund and cancellation checks

The personal pricing page displays a 30-day money-back guarantee. Before purchase, save the checkout terms, renewal amount, billing frequency and cancellation method. A refund guarantee does not eliminate the need to cancel before future renewal.

  • Confirm the billing country and tax
  • Save the renewal date
  • Verify whether a promotion applies only to new users
  • Retain the order confirmation

Which plan is the lowest suitable option?

Choose Free

You use one primary device and want to evaluate saving, generating and filling credentials.

Choose Premium

One person needs synchronized access across computers, phones and browsers.

Choose Family

Two to five people need separate Premium accounts and controlled sharing.
