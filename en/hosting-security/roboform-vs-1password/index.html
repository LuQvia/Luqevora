RoboForm vs 1Password: Price, Free Plan, Security, Sharing and Best Fit

RoboForm emphasizes lower-cost personal plans and detailed form filling. 1Password emphasizes its Secret Key account design, polished vault workflows and broader business and developer ecosystem.

Published and reviewed: July 24, 2026Estimated reading time: 12 minutesBy Luqevora.com Editorial Team

Research method: We reviewed the providers’ official product, pricing, security and help documentation on July 24, 2026. Prices, features, regional availability and promotions can change, so verify the final checkout and terms before purchase.

Bottom line

Choose RoboForm when lower personal cost, a one-device free plan and form filling are the priority. Choose 1Password when its Secret Key model, family vault experience, developer tools or business ecosystem better matches the requirement.

Both products should be tested with the same browser, mobile device and representative logins. Price alone does not show whether autofill, sharing and recovery fit the user’s workflow.

Price and plan structure

RoboForm’s official page displays a lower personal renewal total, while 1Password’s pricing includes a 14-day trial and no permanent free tier. 1Password’s first-year promotional price must be separated from its normal annual cost.

Families should compare account count and sharing workflow, not only the package price. Both products provide separate accounts rather than exposing every family member’s private items.

Security and recovery differences

RoboForm describes AES-256 encryption, PBKDF2 SHA-256 and zero-knowledge infrastructure. 1Password’s account model combines an account password with a Secret Key. These designs are not interchangeable, so users should understand recovery before migration.

  • Enable a strong second factor
  • Test account recovery before storing critical credentials
  • Protect endpoint devices and browser sessions
  • Keep emergency or recovery information outside the vault where appropriate

Autofill, form filling and sharing

RoboForm’s long-standing emphasis is login and web-form filling. 1Password also autosaves and autofills credentials, while its shared vault and ecosystem positioning may fit households and organizations already using its apps and integrations.

Test multi-step logins, payment forms, passkeys, mobile apps and private browsing. The product that succeeds on the actual sites used most often is the stronger fit.

Migration from 1Password to RoboForm

RoboForm publishes a specific import guide for 1Password on Windows, Mac and the browser extension. The process uses a CSV export containing logins and passwords.

The CSV is unencrypted. Delete it after confirming the import, and do not upload or email it.

Which should you choose?

Choose RoboForm

  • Lower published personal renewal cost matters
  • You want a permanent one-device free plan
  • Form filling is a major daily task
  • You want documented import from 1Password

Choose 1Password

  • You prefer its Secret Key account design
  • Developer and business workflows matter
  • You already use its family or shared-vault model
  • Its trial performs better on your devices and sites

Frequently asked questions

Which is cheaper, RoboForm or 1Password?

Based on US annual pricing displayed on July 24, 2026, RoboForm Premium had a lower renewal price than 1Password Individual. Verify current checkout prices and promotions.

Does either service have a permanent free plan?

RoboForm lists a free one-device plan. 1Password advertises a 14-day trial rather than a broad permanent free personal plan.

Can I move from 1Password to RoboForm?

RoboForm publishes a dedicated 1Password export and import guide. The exported CSV is unencrypted and should be deleted after verification.
