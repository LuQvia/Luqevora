Overview

The store registers products, processes orders and checkout on iPad or iPhone, and manages sales, stock, and customers, with optional links to AirPAY, accounting, reservations, and ordering.

Pricing

The free component is the POS software. Calculate the first-year total for the required iPad, receipt printer, cash drawer, barcode reader, cash recycler, AirPAY, and ordering modules.

Best fit

Retail, restaurant, and service businesses that want core checkout and sales management without a recurring POS software fee

Documented strengths

  • Free core POS application
  • Integrated product, inventory, customer, and sales management
  • Connections to AirPAY, accounting, reservations, and ordering services

Cautions before purchase

  • Hardware such as an iPad is separate
  • Cashless payments require a separate application and underwriting
  • Advanced ordering workflows require additional services
