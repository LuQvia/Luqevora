Basecamp

A project collaboration toolkit combining messages, to-dos, card tables, schedules, documents, chat, and client access

2official sources
6related articles

Overview

Each project gathers discussion, to-dos, cards, schedule, and files, with client or external participation limited to relevant spaces.

Pricing

Per-user and flat organization pricing diverge as team size grows. Verify free-project limits, storage, support, and treatment of external clients.

Best fit

Small and growing teams that want calm, centralized project communication without complex configuration

Documented strengths

  • Project-centered information organization
  • Client and external collaboration
  • Choice between per-user and flat organization pricing

Cautions before purchase

  • Advanced dependencies and resource management are limited
  • Detailed reporting may need integrations
  • Capabilities can overlap with existing specialist tools
