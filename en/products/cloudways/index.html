Cloudways

Managed cloud hosting that operates applications and servers on a selected infrastructure provider

2official sources
6related articles

Overview

Servers and applications are managed separately, with staging, SSH, Git, caching, and backups integrated into development operations.

Pricing

Cloudways cost changes with infrastructure, CPU, memory, storage, bandwidth, backups, and CDN. Server-level cost should be allocated across the sites it will host.

Best fit

Developers and agencies that want flexible cloud configuration and multi-site operations

Documented strengths

  • Choice of cloud infrastructure
  • Server and app administration
  • Developer-oriented tools

Cautions before purchase

  • More configuration than shared hosting
  • Add-on cost estimation
  • Email and domains may be separate
