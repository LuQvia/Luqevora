Overview

It collects conversations across conferencing services and turns them into a searchable team library with CRM and workflow integrations.

Pricing

Fireflies.ai uses seat-based tiers with differences in storage, conversation intelligence, and administration. AI credits, add-ons, and auto-upgrade rules should be reviewed as part of cost.

Best fit

Teams in sales, recruiting, or customer work that reuse meeting data across the organization

Documented strengths

  • Search across conversations
  • CRM and workflow integrations
  • Team analytics and conversation intelligence

Cautions before purchase

  • Seat fees and AI credits must be combined
  • Storage varies by tier
  • Recording consent and retention rules are required
