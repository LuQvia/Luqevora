Overview

It can connect forms, email nurturing, sales funnels, webinars, and digital products into one customer journey.

Pricing

GetResponse plan choice depends on list size, billing term, automation depth, webinars, and creator features. A trial is useful for checking feature boundaries.

Best fit

Businesses that want funnels, webinars, or selling workflows in addition to email

Documented strengths

  • Automation and funnels
  • Landing pages
  • Webinar and selling features

Cautions before purchase

  • Price rises with list size
  • Large feature gaps between tiers
  • Broad scope requires setup planning
