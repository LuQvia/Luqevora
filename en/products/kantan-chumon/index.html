A restaurant ordering and operations system combining customer smartphone ordering with a tablet-based POS

3official sources
3related articles

Overview

Customers order from smartphones or tablets while the restaurant manages POS, handheld, ordering, checkout, and sales data; Japanese, Chinese, English, and Korean are supported

Pricing

Hardware, payment processing, installation, configuration, extra devices, and contract conditions depend on the deployment. The official pricing page lists tablet-order add-ons at JPY 880 per device per month, additional handheld devices at JPY 1,100 each, and paid LINE support at JPY 2,200 per month.

Best fit

Restaurants seeking to reduce order waiting, manage limited floor staffing, and support multilingual ordering

Documented strengths

  • Published monthly prices by deployment type
  • Customer smartphone ordering and tablet POS
  • Japanese, Chinese, English, and Korean support
  • Payment-terminal, printer, and accounting integrations

Cautions before purchase

  • A quotation is still required for hardware, payments, installation, and total cost
  • Subsidy eligibility and approval apply
  • Fallback procedures are required for outages
