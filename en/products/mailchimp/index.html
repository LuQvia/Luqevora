Mailchimp

A marketing platform combining email, audience segmentation, automation, forms, and web or commerce integrations

Overview

Contacts are organized into audiences and segments, then connected to campaigns, automations, forms, and commerce data.

Pricing

Mailchimp pricing depends on stored contacts across audiences, monthly send allowances, users, and features. Duplicate contacts and overage behavior should be checked.

Best fit

Businesses that value templates, integrations, and broad marketing features

Documented strengths

  • Large template and integration ecosystem
  • Segmentation and automation
  • Commerce and marketing features

Cautions before purchase

  • Contact billing needs careful review
  • Send and user limits
  • Total cost can rise with scale
