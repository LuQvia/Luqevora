NordVPN

A consumer and small-team security service combining encrypted VPN access with threat and monitoring features across devices

Overview

Users install apps, configure locations and automatic connection, and protect traffic on public networks or while traveling. Device inventory and exception rules should be documented.

Pricing

Check the initial term and bonus period, renewal total, simultaneous devices, bundled password, cloud, identity or data-removal features, and dedicated-IP pricing.

Best fit

Individuals and small users that want VPN access and adjacent security features in one subscription

Documented strengths

  • VPN apps across major operating systems
  • Bundles with threat and monitoring features
  • Broad server-location choice

Cautions before purchase

  • Introductory and renewal prices differ
  • Bundled features vary by region
  • A VPN does not replace device and account security
