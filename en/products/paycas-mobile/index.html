PayCAS Mobile

An Android-based all-in-one mobile payment terminal with an integrated printer, card, e-money, and QR readers, plus a SoftBank SIM

3official sources
8related articles

Overview

Staff carry the SIM-connected terminal, process card, e-money, and QR payments on one device, print receipts, and can add business applications through PayCAS DX Store.

Pricing

The quotation should separate terminal cost, connectivity, monthly fees, brand rates, transaction charges, optional applications, minimum term, and replacement liability.

Best fit

Table service, field service, events, and mobile merchants that prioritize a portable terminal with built-in connectivity and receipt printing

Documented strengths

  • Built-in printer and mobile connectivity
  • Cards, e-money, and QR payments on one device
  • Additional functions through Android applications

Cautions before purchase

  • Pricing requires a quotation
  • Standard settlement is twice monthly
  • POS integration must be checked for the specific system
