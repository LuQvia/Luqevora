Overview

RoboForm combines password generation, saving and autofill with detailed web-form filling, identities, secure sharing and passkey support.

Pricing

Free covers unlimited passwords on one device. Premium and Family are billed annually and add cross-device access and paid-plan features. Verify current promotions and renewal totals at checkout.

Best fit

Individuals and families who prioritize autofill and form filling and prefer a straightforward personal subscription.

Documented strengths

  • Form filling and identity profiles
  • One-device permanent free plan
  • Documented imports from many password managers

Cautions before purchase

  • Free plan is limited to one device
  • Promotional and renewal prices differ
  • Business purchases are excluded from the shared affiliate program
