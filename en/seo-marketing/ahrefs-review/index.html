Bottom line: start with free monitoring for owned sites and expand into competitor and backlink research

Decision summary

Ahrefs

Ahrefs provides free features for verified sites and paid plans that expand unverified-site, keyword and competitor research. A free account includes Site Explorer, Site Audit and Web Analytics for eligible owned sites; Lite and above expand competitor analysis, exports, alerts and custom prompts.

Advantages confirmed from official sources

  • Owned sites can start with a free verified project
  • Free access includes Site Explorer, Site Audit and Web Analytics within stated limits
  • Lite lists five projects, 750 tracked keywords and 100,000 crawl credits
  • Backlink, competitor, organic and AI-search research are available in one platform

Constraints to confirm before purchase

  • Free access mainly centers on sites whose ownership can be verified
  • Unverified-site research and large exports require paid-plan and credit checks
  • Additional users and pay-as-you-go data can create extra charges
  • SEO metrics and traffic estimates are Ahrefs data, not first-party measurements

When it may fit

Suitable when

You want free monitoring for owned sites and the option to expand into competitor sites, backlinks, keywords, rankings and historical data.

Consider alternatives when

You need one dashboard for many marketing channels, want high-volume unverified-site research at no cost, or prefer simpler fixed usage limits.

Frequently asked questions

Does Ahrefs have a free plan?

A free account is available for verified sites, with Site Explorer, Site Audit, Web Analytics and other tools subject to limits.

What is a verified project?

A site whose ownership is confirmed through methods such as Google Search Console, an HTML file, DNS TXT or a meta tag. Ahrefs states that verified projects can be used free.

What are the Lite price and key limits?

The pricing page lists $129 per month, five projects, six months of history, 750 tracked keywords, 100,000 crawl credits and one included user.
