Bottom line: a send-volume-led option combining email, messaging, CRM and automation

Decision summary

Brevo

Brevo offers Free, Starter, Standard, Professional and Enterprise. Free includes 300 daily sends, storage for up to 100,000 contacts and automation for up to 2,000 contacts. Starter begins at US$9/month and Standard at US$18/month. The selected monthly send tier also determines the contact storage allowance.

Officially documented advantages

  • Free includes campaigns, transactional messaging and basic reporting
  • Starter removes the daily sending limit
  • Standard lists unlimited-contact automation and A/B testing
  • Contacts and attributes can be exported to CSV

Constraints to verify

  • Unused Free daily sends do not roll over
  • Starter emails include Brevo branding unless an add-on is purchased
  • Extra users, dedicated IP and SSO have plan or add-on requirements
  • Contact storage varies with the selected send tier

When it may fit

Potential fit

Businesses that want to manage cost primarily by send volume and combine email, messaging, CRM, automation and transactional messaging.

When to compare alternatives

Teams that send very frequently to small lists or require advanced access controls at entry-level prices. Model total cost including send tier and add-ons.

Frequently asked questions

How many emails can the Free plan send?

The official help center states 300 emails per day. Unused sends do not roll over.

What is the main difference between Starter and Standard?

Starter focuses on removing the daily send limit. Standard adds unlimited-contact marketing automation, A/B testing, AI send-time optimization, advanced reporting and a landing page.

Can contacts be exported?

Yes. Contacts and attributes can be exported to CSV for all contacts, lists, segments or filtered selections.
