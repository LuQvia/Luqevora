Trial and migration checklist before signup

Run a small HubSpot Marketing Hub pilot with a success condition and deadline. Classify each result as verified, unverified, or requiring added cost, and save screenshots, export files, and the official URLs checked.

Before cancelling an existing service for HubSpot Marketing Hub, verify record counts, history, attachments, permissions, integrations, and recovery steps after migration.

  • Use the same site, keyword set, or contact sample in both products

  • Verify every required integration and tracking setup
  • Record monthly limits and the process when a limit is reached
