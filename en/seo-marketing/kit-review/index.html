Kit Review: Newsletter, Creator and Creator Pro Pricing and Automation Explained

Conclusion: a candidate for growing a newsletter and digital-product business from a broad free tier

Decision summary

Kit

Kit offers a free Newsletter plan for up to 10,000 active unique subscribers, plus Creator and Creator Pro. The free plan includes broad sending and list-building tools, but limits the account to one basic Visual Automation and one Email Sequence and enables one Kit-managed Recommendation slot. Paid pricing scales with subscriber count.

Free access
Newsletter: up to 10,000 active subscribers
Paid starting price
Creator $39/mo; Creator Pro $79/mo up to 1,000
Automation
Free: 1 basic automation + 1 sequence
Export
Subscriber list as CSV

Advantages documented in official sources

  • Newsletter documents unlimited forms, landing pages and email sends
  • Digital products and subscriptions are documented on the free plan
  • Creator and Creator Pro include the core automation, sequence and API toolset
  • Subscriber data can be exported to CSV

Constraints to check before subscribing

  • The free plan enables one Kit-managed Recommendation slot and Kit receives Paid Recommendation earnings from that slot
  • Free automation is limited to one basic Visual Automation and one Email Sequence
  • Paid price rises with subscriber count and accounts are moved to the next tier after exceeding a limit
  • Creator includes two team members; unlimited team access is a Creator Pro feature

When it may fit

Cases that fit

Consider Kit when newsletters, forms, landing pages, tagging, sequences and digital products need to sit in one creator-focused system. Its structure supports starting on the free plan and expanding automation after the list or workflow becomes more complex.

Cases that justify alternatives

Compare other platforms when strict multi-brand permission controls, enterprise SSO/SCIM, or send-volume-only billing is a primary requirement.

Frequently asked questions

Can Kit be used for free?

The Newsletter plan is free for up to 10,000 active unique subscribers and documents unlimited email sends, forms and landing pages.

Does the free plan include automation?

It includes one basic Visual Automation and one Email Sequence. Unlimited automations and sequences are paid-plan features.

How much do the paid plans cost?

At up to 1,000 subscribers, Creator starts at $39 per month and Creator Pro at $79 per month. Annual billing includes two months free and pricing scales with subscriber count.

Can subscriber data be exported?

Yes. Kit documents exporting the full list or a filtered subset as a CSV file.
