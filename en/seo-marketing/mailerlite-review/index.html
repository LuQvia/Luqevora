MailerLite Review: Free, Comfort and Power Pricing, Send Limits, Automation and Forms

This review covers MailerLite’s post-June-2026 Free, Comfort, Power and Enterprise plans, subscriber counts, send volume, automation, forms, sites, seats, billing changes and exports using official sources only.

Bottom line: a compact option for campaigns, forms, automation and landing pages

Decision summary

MailerLite

MailerLite offers Free, Comfort, Power and Enterprise. Free includes 250 active subscribers, 2,500 monthly emails, two users, three automations, three forms, one website and one landing page. Comfort starts at US$12/month and Power at US$25/month. Pricing scales with active subscribers and send volume.

Officially documented advantages

  • Free includes three email editors, automation, forms and a site
  • Comfort adds templates, dynamic email, AI assistance and smart sending
  • Power lists unlimited users, sends, automations and forms
  • Subscribers, segments and campaign or automation activity can be exported

Constraints to verify

  • The current Free plan is limited to 250 subscribers and 2,500 monthly sends
  • Any address active during the billing cycle counts cumulatively
  • Paid accounts may auto-upgrade when subscriber or send limits are exceeded
  • Custom domains for sites and landing pages require Comfort or Power

When it may fit

Potential fit

Small lists that want campaigns, forms, landing pages, a basic website and automation in one product.

When to compare alternatives

Lists with rapid churn, unpredictable send spikes or a requirement to remain indefinitely within a fixed free allowance. Review cumulative counting and automatic upgrades.

Frequently asked questions

What are the Free plan limits?

The current official limits are 250 active subscribers, 2,500 monthly emails and two users, plus three automations, three forms, one website and one landing page.

Do deleted subscribers stop counting immediately?

An address that was active at any point in the billing cycle counts for that cycle. The count resets on the billing anniversary.

Can subscribers be exported?

Yes. Subscribers, groups, segments and campaign or automation activity can be exported as CSV files.
