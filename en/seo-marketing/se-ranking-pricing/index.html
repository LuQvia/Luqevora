What the 14-day trial includes

The official FAQ lists up to 10 projects, 750 tracked keywords per day, 20 AI prompts per day and AI competitor research for up to three domains. The pricing page states that a card is not required. Confirm data retention and upgrade behavior in the account screen.

Common sources of additional cost

Keywords and seats

Extra tracked keywords and admin seats have separate starting prices. Count every operator who needs direct access.

Content articles

Check included content-marketing articles and the cost of additional capacity.

API and MCP

Standard credits may not cover product integrations or high-volume automation.

Agency features

White-label reporting, lead generation and agency workflows may require a separate pack.

How to verify rank-tracking accuracy

SE Ranking's official FAQ says ranking results are taken from search engines and a cached copy of the result is stored. Manual browser results can differ because search history, language and location personalize results. Align location and device settings and use an incognito window when checking.

Search Console average position and a fixed-condition rank tracker answer different questions. Treat Search Console as real impression data and rank tracking as controlled monitoring rather than expecting identical values.

Five inputs to calculate before subscribing

  1. Number of domains and projects.
  2. Keywords, locations and devices checked each day.
  3. Number of admin users.
  4. Monthly crawl and audit volume.
  5. AI search, articles, API and report add-ons.

Frequently asked questions

Is annual pricing charged monthly?

The page shows a monthly equivalent for annual billing. Confirm the billing date and prepaid amount on the checkout screen.

How do I know whether Core is enough?

Check whether 10 projects, 2,000 daily keywords, one admin seat and six months of history meet the workflow. Growth is the comparison point for collaboration and longer history.

Can the displayed currency change?

Region and checkout settings can affect currency and tax. This article does not convert prices independently.
