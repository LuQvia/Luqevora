03

Official product information and practical checks

Semrush

Semrush's SEO plans combine keyword research, rank tracking, site audits and competitor analysis with AI-search visibility functions. The SEO plan shown on the official pricing page documents five monitored websites, 500 daily tracked keywords and a seven-day free trial.

  • Position Tracking and Site Audit in the same plan
  • Keyword, competitor and backlink research
  • AI-search performance, sentiment and custom-prompt monitoring
  • Higher tiers expand sites, keywords, historical data and optimisation features

Check before purchase: Confirm the required toolkit, projects, tracked keywords, users, AI prompts and any add-on charges.

Ahrefs

Ahrefs documents free features for verified sites, including SEO Dashboard, Site Explorer, Site Audit and Web Analytics. Starter expands access to unverified websites, Keywords Explorer and Rank Tracker. Lite and higher plans add broader competitor research, exports, alerts and custom-prompt tracking for AI answers.

  • Start with free functions for verified owned sites
  • Starter documents 200 credits and one unverified project
  • Site Audit is available for verified projects on Free and Starter
  • Lite and above expand research, exports, API and AI-related access

Check before purchase: Review verified versus unverified usage, credits, added users, export rows, historical data and rank-tracking limits.

Ubersuggest

Ubersuggest offers limited free access for keyword research and advertises daily rank tracking, competitor analysis, site audits, content gaps, backlink opportunities and AI-search research. Official product information also describes a one-time Lifetime offer alongside recurring options.

  • Limited free keyword and SEO research
  • Daily ranking, competitor and audit functions in one product
  • A seven-day trial is advertised
  • Lifetime is a one-time-payment alternative rather than recurring billing

Check before purchase: Confirm daily searches, tracked sites and keywords, competitors, crawl pages, users and the limits attached to a Lifetime purchase.

SE Ranking

SE Ranking provides rank tracking, site audits, keyword and competitor research, backlink monitoring, content, local marketing and client-reporting functions. Its official pricing page lists Core, Growth and Enterprise, with a 14-day trial that does not require a credit card.

  • Rank tracking across major search engines
  • Website, on-page and backlink monitoring
  • Documented integrations with GA4, Search Console and Data Studio
  • AI Search, Agency Pack and API options are available through plans or add-ons

Check before purchase: Confirm projects, admin seats, daily keywords, crawled pages, AI prompts, white labelling and API costs.

How to narrow the options by use case

Monitor one owned site with minimal cost

Test Ahrefs' verified-site free tools, Ubersuggest's limited free access and Mangools Free alongside Search Console, then pay only for missing competitor, history or tracking requirements.

Run rankings, audits and competitors from one operating dashboard

Start with Semrush or SE Ranking and compare projects, refresh schedules, reporting, users and AI-search allowances.

Prioritise backlink and competitor investigation

Compare Ahrefs, Semrush and SE Ranking on unverified-domain research, history, exports and backlink update conditions. Treat differing database values as methodology differences.

Keep keyword research straightforward

Test Ubersuggest and Mangools using their free access, then check whether country, language, difficulty metrics, SERP views and export limits fit the workflow.

Frequently asked questions

Can a business operate with only free SEO tools?

Search Console, GA4 and the free functions documented by these services can cover basic monitoring. Paid limits may be required for competitor research, long-term history, large rank-tracking sets, backlinks and automated reporting.

Why do search volume and difficulty differ between tools?

Platforms use different sources, countries, refresh schedules, estimation methods and difficulty formulas. Use values for relative comparison within one platform rather than treating cross-platform numbers as identical.

Is AI-search monitoring required?

It becomes relevant when a business needs to monitor mentions or citations in AI answers as well as conventional search. Check supported platforms, prompt allowances, refresh rates, citation reporting and whether it is included or sold separately.
