How to shortlist alternatives

Start with the work done every week: rank tracking, site auditing, keyword and competitor research, backlinks or reporting. SE Ranking and Semrush cover broad workflows, Screaming Frog is stronger as a technical crawler, and Mangools focuses on a more streamlined research and ranking workflow.

Use Seobility's current plan structure as the baseline

Seobility lists Basic, Premium and Agency. Its official pricing page shows Basic as free with one project, 1,000 pages per crawl and 10 ranking keywords. Premium lists three projects, 25,000 pages and 300 keywords; Agency lists 15 projects, 100,000 pages and 1,500 keywords.

The official page displays Premium at EUR 49.90 monthly after a 14-day trial and Agency at EUR 179.90 monthly, with annual discounts also shown. Verify VAT, currency, billing cycle and extra-project or keyword pricing.

Migration checklist

  • Export tracked keywords, locations, devices and competitors.
  • Save crawl history and resolved issue lists.
  • Document backlink-monitoring targets and disavow ownership.
  • Run old and new tools in parallel to compare location and device settings.
  • Calculate add-on costs for keywords, seats, API, reports and extra projects.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is closest to Seobility?

SE Ranking is one broad-workflow candidate because it combines rankings, auditing, research and backlinks, but project and usage limits differ. Compare the tasks that matter rather than assuming feature names are equivalent.

Can free tools replace Seobility?

Basic monitoring may be possible for a small site. Multiple domains, history, high keyword volume, scheduled reporting and client access often require paid capacity.

Why do rank results differ between tools?

Location, device, language, timing and search-engine settings can differ. Align settings before comparing results.
