Conclusion: choose funfo for no-fee self-testing and Kantan Chumon for a quoted modular deployment

funfo publishes a zero-fee Free tier and lets a restaurant test the iPad and smartphone-order workflow directly. It fits operators that want to validate one location before moving to a paid tier.

Kantan Chumon publishes a JPY 2,980 monthly POS-only starting point and separate configurations for handheld and ordering workflows. It suits restaurants that want to select a named configuration and confirm hardware, payment, installation, and support in a deployment quotation.

Cost and rollout

With funfo, add the iPad, printers, payment fees, support, and the paid tier needed for concurrent devices or customer management.

Kantan Chumon publishes monthly tax-inclusive prices of JPY 2,980 for POS only, JPY 8,980 for POS plus handheld, JPY 8,980 for web mobile ordering, JPY 12,980 for LINE mobile ordering, and JPY 15,980 for TTO ordering. Confirm hardware, payment fees, installation, extra devices, support, and contract terms separately.

Operations and customer growth

funfo is differentiated by the visible LINE and customer-management path, especially in the higher tier. It is worth testing when the restaurant wants ordering data to support repeat-customer communication.

Kantan Chumon emphasizes smartphone ordering, sales analysis, and recommendation-related functions. Confirm exact plan availability and reporting screens in a demonstration.

Common evaluation checklist

Send both providers the same requirement sheet.

  • First-year and renewal total cost
  • Required devices and printers
  • Peak concurrent orders and logins
  • Menu modifiers, courses, and table movement
  • Multilingual scope
  • Payment and accounting integrations
  • Support and outage procedures
  • Data export and termination terms
  • KPIs: waiting time, average check, table turns, and order errors
