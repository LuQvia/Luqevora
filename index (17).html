Editorial Policy

Our standards for research, evaluation, updates, corrections, and the use of AI.

Luqevora aims to provide accurate, understandable information that supports informed decisions.

Editorial principles

  1. We prioritize primary sources such as official documentation, pricing, and terms.
  2. We distinguish verified facts, testing observations, and editorial judgments where possible.
  3. We include limitations, drawbacks, and non-fit cases—not only benefits.
  4. Affiliate relationships alone do not determine rankings.
  5. Important content includes review or update dates where practical.

Core evaluation areas

Functionality

Core features and use-case fit

Usability

Setup, learning, and operational effort

Pricing

Total cost, free tiers, add-ons, and cancellation

Trust

Security, support, and provider transparency

Use of AI

AI may assist with research organization, translation, or outlining. Human review is required before publication, and AI output is not treated as a verified source by itself.
