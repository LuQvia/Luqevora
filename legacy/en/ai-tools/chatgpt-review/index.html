ChatGPT Review: Pricing, Features and Data Policies

This review explains ChatGPT Free, Plus and Business using official pricing, help and privacy documentation, without claiming a benchmark-based quality ranking.

Published and verified: July 16, 2026Estimated reading time: 10 minutesBy Luqevora.com Editorial

Research method: We reviewed official pricing pages, help documentation, and privacy or security materials on July 16, 2026. Prices, features, and usage limits can change, so verify the checkout and contract immediately before purchase.

Review scope: This is an official-source decision guide. We did not run a reproducible benchmark, so we do not rank answer quality or writing performance.

Disclosure: This article contains no affiliate links at publication. If links are added later, they will be disclosed near the link and under our Affiliate Disclosure.

Bottom line: compare Plus for one person and Business for a managed team of two or more

Decision summary

A broad work assistant covering writing, search, files, analysis, images and voice

Official plan documentation covers drafting, summarization, search, file uploads, data analysis, image generation, voice, Deep Research and custom GPTs. Plus is the individual paid option. Business starts at two users and adds a dedicated workspace and business administration.

Consumer and business data terms are different

Free and Plus

OpenAI says consumer users can control whether new chats are used to improve models. Turning the setting off prevents later conversations from being used for training. Temporary Chat is not saved to history or memory and is not used for training.

Business and Enterprise

OpenAI states that inputs and outputs from ChatGPT Business, Enterprise and other business products are not used for model training by default unless the customer explicitly opts in to share data.

Operational note: “Not used for training” is only one control. Also define retention, connected-app permissions, sharing, offboarding and prohibited data.

Who ChatGPT fits—and when to compare alternatives

Good fit

  • One service for writing, research, files, tables and images
  • Drafting from PDFs, spreadsheets or presentations
  • A managed workspace for a team of two or more
  • Reusable workflows through custom GPTs

Compare alternatives

  • Work must stay primarily inside Google Workspace
  • A five-person-plus team specifically wants Claude connectors and controls
  • The only requirement is specialist English editing and style governance
  • The project needs API access but not a chat subscription

Pre-purchase checklist

  1. Confirm user count: Plus for one individual; compare Business when two or more users need management.
  2. Classify data: Separate public, internal, personal and confidential information.
  3. Review settings: Consumer data controls or Business admin settings.
  4. Minimize connectors: Grant only the Drive, Slack or other permissions required.
  5. Keep human review: Verify facts, numbers, law, rights and customer-facing claims.

Frequently asked questions

What is the difference between ChatGPT Plus and Business?

Plus is designed for an individual. Business starts at two users and adds a dedicated workspace, administration and security features, with organization data excluded from model training by default.

Does ChatGPT Plus include API usage?

No. ChatGPT subscriptions and the OpenAI API are billed separately.

Should a business put confidential data into a personal ChatGPT account?

Review the consumer data controls, internal policy and applicable terms. For recurring confidential workflows, compare a business contract such as ChatGPT Business.
