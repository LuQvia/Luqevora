Core capabilities and day-to-day workflow

It connects calendars and conferencing services to handle meeting capture, transcription, summaries, search, and sharing as one flow.

Do more than inspect a feature list. Test one complete sequence from input through collaboration, approval, and publishing or export. Record points that confuse operators, settings restricted to administrators, and integrations that require reauthentication so rollout effort is visible.

  • Core strength: Real-time transcription
  • Core strength: Searchable meeting notes
  • Core strength: Calendar and conferencing integrations

Pricing model and plan limits

A free individual tier, Pro, and organization-focused Business and Enterprise plans

Otter.ai starts with a free individual tier. Paid plans expand transcription minutes, file imports, and team administration; supported languages and meeting-duration limits also matter.

Identify the lowest tier that meets every required capability and limit, rather than choosing the cheapest headline plan. Normalize introductory and renewal pricing, growth in seats or usage, add-ons, migration, and training to the same evaluation period.

  • Monthly and annual totals, including AI credits
  • Model, message, and file-processing limits
  • Training use, retention, and controls for submitted data
  • Extra charges for APIs, integrations, and team features
