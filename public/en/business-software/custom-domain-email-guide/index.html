Core capabilities and workflow

Register or control the domain, choose a mailbox provider, verify the domain, change MX records, configure authentication, create users and aliases, migrate mail, and monitor delivery.

  • Mailboxes and aliases on the company domain
  • Gmail or another client for sending and receiving
  • SPF, DKIM, and DMARC for authenticated delivery

Pricing and plan limits

Compare per-user mailbox cost, storage, retention, migration, archive, security, support, and administrator time. Hosting-included mail and dedicated business suites have different service levels.

For custom-domain email, identify the lowest plan containing every required feature and compare the total for the full billing period.

  • Record introductory and renewal pricing separately
  • Compare monthly, annual, and longer-term totals
  • Check limits for seats, storage, sites, sends, or usage
  • Review refund, cancellation, and data-retention terms

Strengths and trade-offs

A low-cost mailbox can work for small teams, while identity, retention, legal hold, and administration may justify a dedicated suite.

  • Strength: Professional addresses under company control
  • Strength: Improved security and deliverability when authentication is configured
  • Verify: DNS change and migration downtime
  • Verify: Offboarding, recovery, and application-sender inventory

Who it suits

It fits every organization that can assign clear ownership for the domain and mail administration.

  • Companies moving from personal addresses
  • Teams standardizing accounts and aliases
  • Organizations improving sender authentication

Implementation checklist

Pilot custom-domain email in a small scope and document cost, workflow, migration, and administration before rollout.

  • Define the objective and completion criteria in one sentence
  • Reproduce a representative task in a free plan or trial
  • Test administrator, contributor, and viewer permissions
  • Perform a real backup or export test
  • Record the renewal date and internal owner
