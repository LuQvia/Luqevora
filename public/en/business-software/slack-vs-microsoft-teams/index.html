How to compare price and plan limits

Slack Free limits message history and app connections. Paid evaluation should include seats, history, external sharing, SSO, retention, and AI features.

Teams value differs between standalone and Microsoft 365 bundles. Compare Office apps, OneDrive, email, meeting limits, and phone or room add-ons.

Compare Slack and Microsoft Teams by the annual total of the lowest plan that meets every required limit, not the headline price alone.

  • Monthly and annual totals, including minimum seat commitments
  • Limits for members, guests, storage, and automations
  • Plan differences for permissions, audit controls, and SSO
  • Integration, export, and post-cancellation retention terms

Which one should you choose?

Test routine chat, external collaboration, meetings, file permissions, and employee offboarding with the same scenario.

  • Choose Slack for a broad SaaS ecosystem, channel-centric work, and external organization collaboration
  • Choose Teams for Microsoft 365, meetings, calling, Office coauthoring, and enterprise controls
  • Also compare Google Chat for smaller organizations standardized on Google Workspace

Pre-purchase trial checklist

Run the same sample project in both products and record task time, friction, and extra cost.

  • Reproduce the same project or business workflow in both products
  • Test administrator, contributor, and guest permissions
  • Verify notifications, automations, and required integrations
  • Export history, attachments, and core records
  • Record seat rules, billing units, and renewal dates

Frequently asked questions

Which is easier for beginners, Slack or Microsoft Teams?

Teams is easier to justify when Microsoft 365 is already standard. Slack is often clearer when a team uses many independent SaaS products and channel-based communication.

Can I compare them using free plans?

A Slack or Microsoft Teams free plan or trial can reveal the core workflow, but also check paid-plan limits, administration, export, and support on their official comparison pages.

What should I verify before migrating?

For Slack and Microsoft Teams, verify export formats, history and attachment coverage, permission mapping, integration reauthorization, and the cancellation date for the old service.
