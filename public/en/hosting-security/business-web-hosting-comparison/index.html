When CPI is the better shortlist

CPI is relevant when staging and controlled release are part of the publishing process, when multiple domains need separated environments, or when Japanese business email controls are important.

Check the standard 10 GB SmartRelease backup allowance, the limited SLA scope, and the paid 24-hour phone support option.

When XServer Business is the better shortlist

XServer Business is relevant when the organization wants large shared-server capacity, fourteen days of web, mail and database backups, configuration assistance, website migration, and administrator-user controls.

Separate temporary cashback pricing from regular contract costs and include the setup fee in the first-year total.

When XServer for WordPress is the better shortlist

It fits companies and agencies that host business email elsewhere and want to standardize multiple WordPress sites through WP Panel, staging, and site-specific subaccounts.

It does not provide mailboxes, so include a separate email service in total cost.

When ConoHa WING Business is the better shortlist

ConoHa WING Business publishes Biz Light, Biz Standard and Biz Advanced plans with allocated vCPU and memory. Standard billing is hourly with a monthly cap, no setup fee, and no minimum term.

The discounted WING Pack uses prepaid fixed terms and does not allow cancellation during the selected term. Review the domain benefit and term commitment together.

Pre-purchase checklist

Share the following requirements among the website owner, agency, finance team and IT administrator.

  • First-year and renewal total including setup and domains
  • Backup scope for web, email and databases
  • Restore responsibility and recovery time objective
  • SLA scope and claim procedure
  • Migration, DNS and acceptance-testing responsibilities
  • Administrator roles for internal teams and agencies

Frequently asked questions

What should a company compare first?

Compare total cost, backup scope, restore procedures, SLA coverage, migration assistance, administrator controls, and support hours against the company operating process.

Is the lowest monthly price automatically the best choice?

No. Migration work, downtime, restore labor, email interruption, and staff time can outweigh a small monthly price difference.

What matters when an agency manages the site?

Document staging, release permission, multi-site separation, backup, recovery, DNS changes, contract ownership, and payment responsibility.
