How to compare price and plan limits

Cloudways cost changes with infrastructure, CPU, memory, storage, bandwidth, backups, and CDN. Server-level cost should be allocated across the sites it will host.

Verify first-term and renewal pricing, sites, visits, bandwidth, storage, phone support, plugin updates, WAF, performance features, additional sites, and overages.

Frequently asked questions

Which is easier for beginners, Cloudways or WP Engine?

Best fit for Cloudways: Developers and agencies that want flexible cloud configuration and multi-site operations. Best fit for WP Engine: Businesses and agencies that want specialist WordPress performance, updates, and incident support. Compare both under the same free or trial conditions.

Can I compare them using free plans?

What should I verify before migrating?
