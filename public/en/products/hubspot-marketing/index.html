Overview

Teams start from CRM contacts, connect forms, pages, email, ads, and campaigns, and use behavior data for automation and revenue reporting.

Pricing

Annualize the base tier with marketing-contact bands, extra seats, send limits, AI credits, required onboarding, and overlap with other Hubs.

Best fit

Growing organizations that want customer data, sales, marketing, and analytics on one platform

Documented strengths

  • CRM-centered unified data
  • Continuity from acquisition to revenue reporting
  • Expansion from small-business to enterprise editions

Cautions before purchase

  • Contacts, seats, and credits can raise cost
  • Higher tiers may require onboarding
  • Broad adoption needs operating design and governance
