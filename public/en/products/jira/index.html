Overview

Teams capture work as issues and manage it through backlogs, boards, sprints, workflows, releases, and dashboards.

Pricing

Budget user count together with automation runs, storage, audit logs, sandboxing, cross-team planning, support, and any other Atlassian products.

Best fit

Organizations standardizing complex development, IT, or multi-team workflows and issue tracking

Documented strengths

  • Flexible issue and workflow configuration
  • Agile planning and release management
  • Atlassian and developer-tool ecosystem

Cautions before purchase

  • Configuration can become complex
  • Automation and administration vary by tier
  • Non-technical teams may need simpler terminology
