Microsoft Copilot

An AI assistant spanning web chat and creation or analysis inside Microsoft 365 applications

Overview

Work can begin in chat and continue inside eligible Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, Teams, and other Microsoft 365 experiences.

Pricing

Individual and organization options differ in included apps, storage, AI allowances, data protection, and prerequisite licenses. Model the annual total together with any existing Microsoft 365 subscription.

Best fit

Individuals and organizations centered on Microsoft 365 documents, spreadsheets, email, and meetings

Documented strengths

  • Integration across Microsoft 365 apps
  • Individual and business options
  • Workflows grounded in organization data

Cautions before purchase

  • Several products and license combinations share the Copilot name
  • Capabilities vary by market, language, and subscription
  • Business use may require a qualifying base license
