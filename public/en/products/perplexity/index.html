Overview

It moves from a question to web retrieval, source inspection, follow-up research, and a structured answer or report. Important conclusions still require opening the primary source.

Pricing

The free experience covers basic search, while advanced models, research, file or deliverable creation, and organization controls vary by plan. Check credits, auto-refill, and regional terms at checkout.

Best fit

People and teams prioritizing cited web research and fast information synthesis

Documented strengths

  • Direct path from answers to cited sources
  • Search and follow-up questions in one flow
  • Options from individual research to organization use

Cautions before purchase

  • Long production workflows may fit another tool better
