Zoom Workplace

A collaboration platform centered on video meetings and extending into chat, phone, whiteboards, documents, scheduling, and AI assistance

2official sources
2related articles

Overview

Teams schedule and host meetings, share screens, record or summarize, continue in chat, and route follow-up work inside Zoom Workplace or connected apps.

Pricing

Combine host licenses, meeting duration, participant capacity, cloud recording, AI, phone, webinar, room, storage, and contract term.

Best fit

Organizations prioritizing accessible external video meetings and a broad ecosystem of meeting-related products

Documented strengths

  • Familiar meeting experience for external participants
  • Expansion across meetings, chat, phone, and webinars
  • Recording, summaries, and integrations

Cautions before purchase

  • Many add-ons can obscure total cost
  • The free tier has meeting constraints
  • Capabilities can overlap with Microsoft or Google environments
