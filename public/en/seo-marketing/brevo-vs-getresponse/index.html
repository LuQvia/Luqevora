How to compare price and plan limits

Brevo cost is influenced more by send volume than list size. Transactional email, dedicated IP, extra users, SMS, and other modules may add cost.

GetResponse plan choice depends on list size, billing term, automation depth, webinars, and creator features. A trial is useful for checking feature boundaries.

Compare Brevo and GetResponse by the annual total of the lowest plan that meets every required limit, not the headline price alone.

  • Monthly and annual totals, including add-ons
  • Limits for projects, keywords, crawls, and sends
  • Data refresh frequency, history, and regional coverage
  • Terms for extra users, reports, APIs, and exports

Frequently asked questions

Which is easier for beginners, Brevo or GetResponse?

Best fit for Brevo: Organizations with larger contact lists that can manage cost through send frequency. Best fit for GetResponse: Businesses that want funnels, webinars, or selling workflows in addition to email. Compare both under the same free or trial conditions.

Can I compare them using free plans?

A Brevo or GetResponse free plan or trial can reveal the core workflow, but also check paid-plan limits, administration, export, and support on their official comparison pages.

What should I verify before migrating?

For Brevo and GetResponse, verify export formats, history and attachment coverage, permission mapping, integration reauthorization, and the cancellation date for the old service.
