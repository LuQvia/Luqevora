Decision summary

GetResponse

GetResponse offers Free, Starter, Marketer, Creator and custom-priced Enterprise. Standard paid plans use list-size tiers from 1,000 to 100,000 contacts and document unlimited monthly email sends. Starter includes one workflow, Marketer adds unlimited automation and ecommerce, and Creator adds webinars, courses and paid newsletters.

Free access
Up to 500 contacts and 2,500 newsletters/mo
Paid starting price
Starter $19/mo at 1,000 contacts
Automation
Starter 1 workflow; Marketer/Creator unlimited
Export
Contacts, reports and other data

Advantages documented in official sources

  • A 14-day premium trial is available without a card
  • Standard paid plans document unlimited monthly email sends
  • Marketer includes unlimited automation, advanced segmentation and abandoned-cart recovery
  • Creator adds webinars, a website builder, courses and paid newsletters

Constraints to check before subscribing

  • Price rises with list size as well as plan level
  • Starter is limited to one workflow with up to six elements
  • Free adds GetResponse branding and limits landing-page traffic and other features
  • Displayed prices are net amounts and local taxes may apply

When it may fit

Cases that fit

Consider GetResponse when email, forms, landing pages, automation and ecommerce need to expand in stages. Creator can also combine courses, webinars and paid newsletters in the same plan.

Cases that justify alternatives

Compare narrower platforms when long-term free capacity or a simple email-only workflow is more important than the broader feature set.

Frequently asked questions

Can GetResponse be used for free?

The Free account remains available and the official page documents up to 500 contacts, 2,500 newsletters per month, basic email marketing, landing pages and forms.

Is a card required for the free trial?

No. The 14-day premium-feature trial is documented as card-free. Without an upgrade, the account returns to the Free limitations.

Does Starter include advanced automation?

Starter supports one custom or template workflow with up to six elements. Unlimited workflows and ecommerce elements are Marketer-or-higher features.

Can contact data be exported?

Yes. Active contacts can be exported with list, subscription, tag, custom-field, scoring and other attributes.
