Which one should you choose?

Run one representative workflow centered on simple newsletters, forms, and pages versus cross-team customer operations around a CRM in both products, then record completion time, revisions, and administrator setup.

  • Choose MailerLite: Creators and small businesses that want straightforward newsletters and automation
  • Choose HubSpot Marketing Hub: Growing organizations that want customer data, sales, marketing, and analytics on one platform
  • Also test Mailchimp for this requirement: Businesses that value templates, integrations, and broad marketing features

Pre-purchase trial checklist

Run the same sample project in both products and record task time, friction, and extra cost.

  • Use the same site, keyword set, or contact sample in both products
  • Check data timestamps and collection coverage
  • Test reports, CSV exports, and sharing permissions
  • Verify every required integration and tracking setup
  • Record monthly limits and the process when a limit is reached

Frequently asked questions

Which is easier for beginners, MailerLite or HubSpot Marketing Hub?

Best fit for MailerLite: Creators and small businesses that want straightforward newsletters and automation. Best fit for HubSpot Marketing Hub: Growing organizations that want customer data, sales, marketing, and analytics on one platform. Compare both under the same free or trial conditions.

Can I compare them using free plans?

A MailerLite or HubSpot Marketing Hub free plan or trial can reveal the core workflow, but also check paid-plan limits, administration, export, and support on their official comparison pages.

What should I verify before migrating?

For MailerLite and HubSpot Marketing Hub, verify export formats, history and attachment coverage, permission mapping, integration reauthorization, and the cancellation date for the old service.
