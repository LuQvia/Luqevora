Verdict: a candidate for teams that want rankings, audits, competitive research and AI-search monitoring in one workflow

Decision summary

SE Ranking

SE Ranking combines rank tracking, keyword and competitor research, backlink analysis, website and on-page audits, local SEO, reporting and AI-search visibility. Its current standard tiers are Core, Growth and Enterprise, with optional capacity and add-ons for seats, keywords, API access and agency workflows.

Advantages confirmed by official sources

  • Rank tracking, research, backlinks and audits in one platform
  • Core lists 10 projects and 2,000 tracked keywords per day
  • Integrations with GA4, Search Console, Looker Studio and Matomo
  • Explicit AI-prompt and AI-competitor research limits in standard plans

Constraints to verify before purchase

  • Annual pricing is shown as a discounted monthly equivalent and is prepaid
  • Extra seats, keywords, articles, locations and agency features can add cost
  • Core and Growth differ in history, guest links, page-change monitoring and support
  • Third-party volume and traffic estimates should not be treated as Search Console measurements

Who should consider it

Good fit

Teams managing multiple sites or clients that want rankings, audits, competitive research, backlinks and AI-search monitoring in one interface. Estimate whether Core capacity is sufficient or whether Growth's history and collaboration features are required.

Consider alternatives when

You only need basic checks for one owned site or a single low-cost research function. Search Console, GA4 and focused free tools may cover part of the workflow.

Frequently asked questions

Can SE Ranking be tried for free?

The official pricing page offers a 14-day trial with no credit card required. It includes up to 10 projects, 750 tracked keywords per day, 20 AI prompts per day and AI competitor research for up to three domains.

What is the main difference between Core and Growth?

Core lists 10 projects, one admin seat, 2,000 daily keywords and 100 daily prompts. Growth lists 30 projects, three admin seats, 5,000 daily keywords and 250 daily prompts, plus lifetime project history, guest links and page-change monitoring.

Are there costs beyond the displayed plan price?

Extra admin seats, keywords, content articles, locations, the Agency Pack, AI Search and API capacity may require add-ons or separate charges. Confirm the limits and add-ons needed before checkout.
