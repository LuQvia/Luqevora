Bottom line: a candidate for managing SEO and AI-search visibility in one platform

Decision summary

Semrush

Semrush combines keyword research, rank tracking, site audits, competitor and backlink research with AI-search brand visibility under the same plan structure. Starter lists five websites, 500 tracked keywords per day and AI-prompt tracking. Buyers should separately confirm websites, keywords, users, reports and add-ons.

Advantages confirmed from official sources

  • Free includes one demo project and 10 reports per day across core tools
  • Starter lists five websites and 500 tracked keywords per day
  • Rank tracking, site audits, competitor and backlink research are available in one environment
  • SEO plans include AI-search visibility and prompt tracking

Constraints to confirm before purchase

  • Annual-equivalent and monthly pricing differ; tax and currency must be checked
  • Additional users, reports and other toolkits may cost extra
  • Higher plans expand sites, keywords and AI prompts, so requirements should be calculated first
  • Metrics are based on Semrush data and estimates and may not match first-party Search Console data

When it may fit

Suitable when

You want SEO and AI-search monitoring in one environment, regularly track multiple sites or many keywords, and need competitor, backlink and technical analysis together.

Consider alternatives when

You only need basic checks for one owned site, have a limited budget or need a single specialist function. Search Console and free tools may cover part of the workflow.

Frequently asked questions

Can Semrush be used free?

A Free plan is listed with no card required, one demo project and 10 reports per day across core tools. Confirm paid-plan limits for ongoing tracking and multiple sites.

What does Starter track?

The official pricing page lists five websites, 500 keywords per day, 50 AI prompts per day and analysis for one AI-visibility domain.

Are there costs beyond the displayed plan price?

Additional users, reports, lead-generation features and other toolkits may be separate charges. Review the checkout and add-ons before purchase.
