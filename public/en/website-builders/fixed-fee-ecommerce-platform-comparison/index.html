XServer Shop cost structure

XServer Shop publishes a JPY 11,000 setup fee on each plan and a Basic monthly equivalent from JPY 1,980 on the longest term. The platform sales commission is zero, while the bundled Stripe path and payouts have separate fees.

Add compatible plugin, template, design, implementation, migration, and ongoing operations. Use the thirty-day trial before choosing a prepaid contract term.

Model by sales volume and workflow

Use monthly sales, orders, average order value, card share, refunds, payout frequency, applications, development, maintenance, and staffing. Payment costs scale with sales, while payout costs depend partly on frequency.

At low sales, setup and subscription can dominate. At high sales, variable payment or transaction fees grow. Fixed-fee platforms can still cost more if technical operations require additional personnel or agencies.

Maintenance and security ownership

XServer Shop manages the base hosting environment and supplied EC-CUBE maintenance, while store operations and detailed EC-CUBE usage remain merchant responsibilities. WooCommerce shifts broader WordPress, server, extension, backup, and security duties to the chosen team and providers.

Shopify and Wix reduce core infrastructure work but require diligence around applications, themes, data export, account changes, and external integrations.

Document which party owns tax updates, accessibility checks, fraud review, data retention, customer-service workflows, and incident communications. These operational duties can outweigh a small difference in platform fees, especially when several external applications or agencies are involved.

Evaluation checklist

Build the same representative catalog and test variants, tax, shipping, payment, refunds, customers, inventory, permissions, CSV, APIs, email, SEO, and exports. Create one annual cost sheet covering every subscription, payment, payout, application, theme, domain, mail, agency, and maintenance item.

Frequently asked questions

Does zero platform commission mean no ecommerce fees?

No. Payment processing, payouts, applications, hosting, development, and operations can still create material cost.

Are fixed-fee platforms always better at scale?

No. Technical staffing and maintenance can outweigh lower platform commissions.
