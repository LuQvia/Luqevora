How to compare price and plan limits

Annualize the site plan together with additional editors, localization, testing, advanced hosting, and AI credits. Verify custom-domain publishing rules on the free tier.

Webflow separates hosting for each published site from workspace collaboration. Its May 2026 plan changes and AI credits make it important to distinguish new pricing from legacy subscriptions.

Frequently asked questions

Which is easier for beginners, Framer or Webflow?

Best fit for Framer: Designers and lean teams building polished marketing sites with a fast path to publishing. Best fit for Webflow: Agencies and design teams that value fine visual control and a structured CMS. Compare both under the same free or trial conditions.

Can I compare them using free plans?

A Framer or Webflow free plan or trial can reveal the core workflow, but also check paid-plan limits, administration, export, and support on their official comparison pages.

What should I verify before migrating?

For Framer and Webflow, verify export formats, history and attachment coverage, permission mapping, integration reauthorization, and the cancellation date for the old service.
