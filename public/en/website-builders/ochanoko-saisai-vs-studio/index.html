Verdict: store administration or design and CMS control

Ochanoko Saisai is faster for predefined store information, coupons, and calendars. Studio is better suited to custom visual design, CMS collections, and collaborative production.

Compare who builds the site and who performs weekly updates after publication.

Different design models

Ochanoko Saisai provides predefined management fields so non-designers can update local-business content. Studio uses a visual canvas and CMS models, providing more freedom but requiring greater initial design and governance work.

Studio plans differ by pages, CMS models and items, form responses, version history, redirects, and other controls. Ochanoko Saisai differs mainly by domain, storage, pages, email, and advertising.

SEO and maintenance

Both can support core metadata, custom domains, and analytics. Studio plan levels affect redirects, custom code, and CMS limits, while Ochanoko Saisai provides page metadata and external analytics support.

When an agency builds in Studio, design update permissions and CMS input views carefully. Ochanoko Saisai can lower training effort for a small store because its fields are predefined.

Comparing total cost

For Ochanoko Saisai, add the plan, domain setup, and annual domain fee. For Studio, add the project plan, any workspace cost, production, CMS design, and maintenance.

When store information changes weekly, update time and governance can matter more than the lowest monthly subscription.

Production responsibility and governance

Studio can support a designer-led build with controlled CMS inputs, while Ochanoko Saisai is closer to a predefined operating system for store staff. The better choice depends on whether the business can maintain a custom design system after launch.

Document who owns the project, domain, analytics, form notifications, and billing. A visually strong site can still become difficult to operate when those responsibilities are left with a former contractor.

Checks before contracting and after launch

During the 30-day trial, place real copy, photographs, menus, store information, and enquiry routes, then test both mobile output and routine administration. Continued use requires completing the contract and payment process from the administration screen.

A proprietary builder rarely transfers its design and functions directly to another CMS. Keep original copy, images, price tables, staff data, and a URL inventory outside the platform, then retest phone links, forms, maps, and analytics after major changes.

Frequently asked questions

Can Ochanoko Saisai build an online store?

The official registration page states that the service does not include a shopping cart. Compare a separate ecommerce platform when products must be sold online.

Is there a free trial?

The provider offers a 30-day trial after full registration. Continued use requires completing the contract and payment process.

Can it use a custom domain?

The Advanced plan supports a custom domain. Include the setup charge and any annual registration fee in the total cost.
