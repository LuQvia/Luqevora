Frequently asked questions

Is the cheapest Shopify plan sufficient?

Even when the cheapest Shopify plan supports basic testing, it may not include the required limits, permissions, history, exports, integrations, or support. Price the lowest tier that meets every mandatory requirement.

Who is Shopify best for?

It is a strong candidate for businesses for which commerce is central and operational scale matters. Verify that the objective—completing the required daily workflow on the intended plan—can be completed with realistic sample data and the required permissions.
