Wix vs Squarespace: Design, Pricing, Commerce, and Site Operations

Wix prioritizes free-form site building and feature breadth; Squarespace prioritizes design consistency and a more guided editing system.

Published / verified: July 19, 2026By Luqevora Editorial Team
Key decision

Decision summary

Choose Wix when precise visual placement and a wide set of business apps are priorities. Choose Squarespace when a polished template system and consistent content operations matter more than free-form layout.

  • Wix: free-form visual placement, AI setup, and broad business apps
  • Squarespace: structured sections, cohesive templates, content, scheduling, and commerce
  • Both: plan-specific domains, storage, payments, collaborators, and add-ons

Core capabilities and workflow

Both are hosted builders with domains, templates, blogging, commerce, forms, bookings, analytics, and marketing options, but they constrain design differently.

Pricing and plan limits

Compare annual and monthly billing, domain renewal, email, scheduling, commerce terms, apps, collaborators, and migration work.

For Wix and Squarespace, identify the lowest plan containing every required feature and compare the total for the full billing period.

  • Record introductory and renewal pricing separately
  • Compare monthly, annual, and longer-term totals
  • Check limits for seats, storage, sites, sends, or usage
  • Review refund, cancellation, and data-retention terms

Strengths and trade-offs

Wix freedom can create inconsistent layouts; Squarespace's consistency can feel restrictive for highly custom placement.

  • Strength: Both can launch a polished site without server management
  • Strength: The editing-model difference is easy to test
  • Verify: Regional plans and commerce terms
  • Verify: Export coverage and rebuild effort when leaving

Who it suits

Have the future editor recreate and update one representative page in each platform.

  • Wix: feature-rich small-business sites
  • Squarespace: portfolios, services, and brand-led sites
  • Owners who value managed hosting and simple updates

Implementation checklist

Pilot Wix and Squarespace in a small scope and document cost, workflow, migration, and administration before rollout.

  • Define the objective and completion criteria in one sentence
  • Reproduce a representative task in a free plan or trial
  • Test administrator, contributor, and viewer permissions
  • Perform a real backup or export test
  • Record the renewal date and internal owner
